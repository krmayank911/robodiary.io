--- layout: proximity_layout title: IR/Proximity Sensors meta: our different types of proximity sensors. --- An Infra-Red (IR) sensor uses an IR transmitter and an IR receiver, which can be used as a color contrast differentiator and a proximity sensor, depending upon the potentiometer configuration.
The IR transmitter transmits the IR light on the surface and depending upon the color of the surface, some amount of the IR light is reflected back and received by the IR receiver, whose output is given to a comparator along with the output from the potentiometer, to get HIGH signal, if the surface is white and LOW if it is black.
