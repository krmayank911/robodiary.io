--- layout: moisturesensor_layout title: Soil Moisture Sensor meta: A sensor that measures the moisture in the soil. --- Soil moisture sensor is used to measure the water content in soil. The soil moisture sensor uses capacitance to measure the dielectric permitivity of the surrounding medium.
In soil, dielectric permitivity is a function of water content.The sensor creates a small voltage proportional to the dielectric permitivity of soil. This small analog voltage signal is fed to microcontroller to process and to calculate the water content in soil.
